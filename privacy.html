Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 29, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Crypto API Extension. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our extension.

2. Information We Collect

Our extension collects the following types of information:

  • Favorite Cryptocurrencies: The symbols and names of cryptocurrencies you mark as favorites.
  • Alert Settings: Customized price alerts including price thresholds and conditions.
  • Custom Sounds: Audio files you upload for alert notifications.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or financial details.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide real-time cryptocurrency price monitoring.
  • Enable you to set and manage personalized price alerts.
  • Enhance your user experience by storing your preferences and settings.

4. Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored locally using Chrome’s storage.local API. This ensures that your data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties. We implement best practices to protect your data from unauthorized access and ensure its integrity.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our extension, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your information.
  • Delete your data at any time.

To manage your data, simply use the extension’s interface to modify your favorites, alerts, or remove custom sounds.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
